Report a Sighting

Help the sonde community by reporting where you found or spotted a radiosonde. Your reports help us recover more sondes and improve our data.

What to report
  • Found a weather balloon or radiosonde on the ground
  • Saw one landing or hanging from a tree/power line
  • Have the physical device and want to return it
Safety notice
  • Do NOT open or disassemble the device
  • Do NOT attempt to retrieve from power lines
  • Handle with clean gloves if possible
